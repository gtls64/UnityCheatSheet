Audio

Play audio from an AudioSource using the AudioSource.Play method.

public AudioSource audioSource;
audioSource.Play();

Adjust the volume of an AudioSource using the AudioSource.volume property.

audioSource.volume = 0.5f;

Play a sound effect using the AudioSource.PlayOneShot method.

public AudioClip soundEffect;
audioSource.PlayOneShot(soundEffect);
